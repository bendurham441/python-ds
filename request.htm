Adrian Peterson

Adrian Lewis Peterson  (All Day; Purple Jesus)

Position: RB

6-1, 220lb (185cm, 99kg)

Born: March 21, 1985 in Palestine, TX

College: Oklahoma  (College Stats)

Weighted Career AV (100-95-...): 101 (138th overall since 1960)

High School: Palestine (TX)
